Sports Day

At NSBM we believe that all work and no play makes one dull, therefore, our students are encouraged to de-stress themselves by taking part and showing off their amazing multi-talented abilities at the annual sports day.



Crico Fiesto

Crico Fiesto is a fun filled cricket fest for students of NSBM. Our students showcased that they can indeed excel in almost anything!



Siyapath Siya Udanaya

The Sinhala and Tamil New Year is one of the most colorful events in the NSBM calendar, organized by the students and faculty members on an annual basis, showcasing culture at its best. This year too, Awurudu was celebrated in a very traditional manner interspersed with fun and frolic at the Colts Cricket Club, Colombo on the 30th of April, 2013.

This gala event was graced by the Chief of Staff (Army) Daya Rathnayake as the Chief Guest with Dr.E.A. Weerasinghe - CEO, NSBM, Mr. D.M.A. Kulasooriya – Dean, School of Business and Mrs. Kishani Wijesiriwardana – Dean, School of Computing among the most honored guests. Celebrated artistes, Mrs. Pradeepa Dharmadasa and Ms. Ureni Noshika too graced the occasion as esteemed members of the panel of judges for the highlight of the bright evening, the crowning of the glittering and glamorous Awurudhu ‘Kumariya’ and ‘Kumaru’.



Inspiro

Inspiro’ 2013 held for the first time this year is a workshop on entrepreneurship organized by the BSc in Business Management (Special), first year students. The theme for this year was, “Be the change-Make a difference” in order to accomplish the module Critical Enabling and Skills Training (CREST). The event saw presentations, role plays, and guest speeches by world renowned entrepreneurs including, Dr. Ravi Liyanage, CEO of the Kingdom of Raigam and Mr. Bathiya Jayakody and Mr. Santhush Weeraman, BnS partners.

Student clubs

Rotaract Club

The Rotaract Club of NSBM is one of the 5 Rotaract clubs sponsored by the Rotary Club of Colombo West. The Club was Chartered and Sponsored by us in 2013 and has a membership of 84.

The club was chartered on the 1st of October 2013 with Adheesha Dharmakeerthi leading the club as its first president. The club is one of the most dynamic in the district with a current membership of over 120 which is commendable considering the club is just one year old.
