NSBM Green University Town-At the end the dream came true...


The NSBM Green University Town is scheduled to be declared open by His Excellency the president Maithripala Sirisena, along with Hon Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe, by the invitation of Minister of Skills Development and Vocational Training, Hon Mahinda Samarasinghe and State Minister of Skills Development and Vocational Training, Hon Palitha Range Bandara.

This state-of-the-art modern university is the first and the only of it's kind in the whole of South Asia and will hold 30,000 undergraduates at a given time.

Breaking the ground rules of higher education, NSBM has initiated the construction of Sri Lanka’s first ever Green University Township making Sri Lanka one of the main educational hubs in the world. Inspired with the vision to make Sri Lanka the ‘Knowledge Hub’ of Asia, NSBM is geared with exploring innovativeness in teaching, learning, research and extracurricular activities facilitated by the remarkable infrastructure that will be completed in 2015.

NSBM-BOC Flexible Financial Scheme


>Turning a new leaf in Sri Lankan Higher Education, the NSBM Green University Town is set to open its doors 26th October to the youth of Sri Lanka. A media briefing was held today headed by the Minister of Skills Development and Vocational Training, Hon Mahinda Samarasinghe at the auditorium of the ministry to inform of the flexible financial scheme for Green University Town students, by the Bank of Ceylon. Designed specifically for the students of the NSBM Green University Town, this scheme lets students pay only the interest in a monthly payment scheme, for a maximum of 4 years of study period, and the total amount could be repaid in 7 years.
This is the very first customized financial scheme introduced by BOC to an education institute in Sri Lanka. Up to 80% of the course fee, subjected to a maximum of Rs 2 million could be obtained as the loan, for any local or foreign degree awarded by NSBM Green University Town, or affiliated Foreign University. Exchange of the document for this special agreement happened between Vice Chancellor of NSBM Green University Town, Dr E A Weerasinghe and Mr. D. P. K. Gunasekara - Deputy General Manager, Corporate & Offshore Banking of Bank of Ceylon and Mr. C Amarasinghe - Deputy General Manager, Retail Banking of Bank of Ceylon.

President's Visit to NSBM Green University Town

NSBM Green University Town, the first and the only one of it's kind in the whole of South Asia is to be inaugurated very soon. Today HE The President paid a visit to the facility and there was a tree plantation done as a remembrance of the event as well. HE The President voiced his ideas about the venture, and was of the opinion that this is an ideal gateway for the Sri Lankan youth to shine, within the country as well as internationally.

President Maithripala Sirisena undertakes an inspection tour at the NSBM green university town which is being constructed at Pitipana in Homagama. It is the only green university in South Asia. The President inquired the construction work this afternoon. The university town is built in a land block that extends to 26 acres. The construction activities are being carried out under the purview of the Ministry of Skills Development and Vocational Training. The estimated budget for the construction is 10.2 billion rupees. 30,000 students can be accommodated at the university. Computer sciences, management and engineering will be taught. More than 50 degree courses have been introduced that go with demands of the job market locally and internationally. It is aim to launch the NSBM green university town at the end of next month. The President planted a sapling to mark his visit at the university town.

NSBM Officials visit China

NSBM Green University Town officials along with the Vice-Chancellor, with a delegation from the Ministry of Skills Development and Vocational Training, visited China, in order to further discuss on having foreign students from Beijing Foreign Studies University, to study at NSBM Green University Town.

School of Engineering

NSBM is working towards offering Degree programmes in Engineering. Initially, these programmes will be offered in the following three disciplines: · Electrical and Electronic Engineering · Mechatronic Engineering · Computer Systems Engineering These programmes will be offered with the approval of the Ministry of Higher Education, as well as in collaboration with reputed overseas universities. NSBM plans to launch these programmes in its' new Green University Town, Pitipana, Homagama, in 2017. Watch this space for further information…

Visit to Stockholm University

The Department of Computer and Systems Sciences and Stockholm University was visited by the Vice Chancellor of the National School of Business Management (NSBM) last week. A new partnership is being formed with the Stockholm University, Sweden by the National School of Business Management (NSBM), working with the Department of Computer and Systems Sciences. The programme covers a capacity-building project for doctoral education at NSBM with Stockholm University providing guidance. Because the doctoral students will do most of their work in Sri Lanka, remote guidance will be the key. All of the prospective Ph.D.s are teachers at NSBM.

NSBM Corporate Planning session

NSBM employees participated in a workshop to come up with a sound corporate plan for the next years to come. NSBM will be moving in to the Green University by October 2016.
